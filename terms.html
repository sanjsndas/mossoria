Terms of Privacy & Use

Welcome to Mossoria Ivy Sanctum. By using our journal, you agree to honor the roots of this community — respect, transparency, and a shared love for the living world. Below are the guiding principles of our digital sanctuary.

01 Our commitment to your data

We collect only what is necessary to nurture your experience. Your personal information is never sold, traded, or used beyond the purpose of communication and service improvement.

  • Minimal collection: name, email, and voluntary preferences.
  • Secure storage: encrypted databases and restricted access.
  • No third-party sharing: except when required by law or with explicit consent.
🌱 We treat your data like a seedling — with care, and only for growth.

02 Use of the sanctum

Our content is offered for personal, educational, and non-commercial use. You are welcome to share articles with attribution, but reproduction or distribution for profit requires prior permission.

  • Attribution: always credit Mossoria Ivy Sanctum and the author.
  • Non-commercial: no reselling or repackaging of our resources.
  • Community respect: engage with kindness and curiosity.

If you wish to use our work in a way not covered here, reach out — we’re reasonable folk.

03 Intellectual property & contributions

All original text, illustrations, and designs are the property of Mossoria Ivy Sanctum and its contributors. Guest writers retain rights to their individual work, but grant us a license to publish and archive.

  • User-generated content: comments, submissions, or feedback may be used for editorial purposes.
  • Attribution: we always credit authors and photographers.

If you believe your work has been used improperly, please contact us and we will resolve it promptly.

04 Privacy & cookies

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience — never for tracking or advertising. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time.

  • Essential cookies: for site functionality and preferences.
  • Analytics: anonymized, aggregated data to improve content.
  • No tracking: we do not use cross-site tracking or retargeting.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy and Cookies Policy.

05 Changes & updates

We may update these terms occasionally to reflect new practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on the site.
