From a single seed

Mossoria Ivy Sanctum was born from a love of forgotten gardens, heirloom varieties, and the quiet wisdom of soil. We are a sanctuary for curious growers — whether you have a windowsill or five acres.

Our values →

Our mission

To cultivate a living archive of botanical knowledge — blending ancestral practices with regenerative science. We believe gardening is a form of storytelling, and every plant holds a lesson.

  • 🌱 Preserve heirloom seeds & traditions
  • 🌿 Promote soil-first, low-impact methods
  • 📖 Share accessible, deep-dive resources

Our story

What started as a small community garden log in 2026 quickly grew into a global journal. We’ve collaborated with ethnobotanists, permaculturists, and backyard growers to create a space that feels both scholarly and soulful.

Mossoria is named after the ancient moss gardens of the northern forests — where ivy and stone hold memory.
