Cookies Policy

Just like a forest floor, our website uses tiny, harmless crumbs — cookies — to make your experience richer. Here’s everything we leave behind, and why.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and keep the sanctum running smoothly. Think of them as little botanical markers that help us tend to your visit.

How we use cookies

We keep our cookie garden minimal and respectful. We use only what is necessary and beneficial:

  • Essential cookies — these are strictly necessary for the site to function (e.g., remembering your consent settings).
  • Analytics cookies — we use a lightweight, anonymized analytics tool (no third-party tracking) to see which pages are most loved, so we can grow more of what you enjoy.
  • Preference cookies — if you choose to opt in, we save your language or theme preferences (though we keep things simple).

Your choices

You are in full control of your cookie experience. You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies, or use the preference toggles below (they reflect your current choice).

Third-party cookies

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, social media trackers, or any external tracking scripts. Our only external links are to our Bluesky and Mastodon profiles — those platforms have their own cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

Managing cookies in your browser
