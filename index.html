    • Frequently asked questions

    What is the best soil pH for vegetables?

    Most vegetables thrive in 6.0–7.0. Acid lovers like blueberries prefer 4.5–5.5.

    How often should I water raised beds?

    Deep watering 2–3 times per week in summer, depending on rainfall. Check 2 inches down.

    Can I compost citrus peels?

    Yes — but balance with carbon-rich materials and turn the pile regularly.
